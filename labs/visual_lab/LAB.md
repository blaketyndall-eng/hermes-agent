# Visual Lab Operating Principles

## Research first

The lab exists to understand visual behavior before building products.

Avoid:

- presets
- gimmick filters
- fake film overlays
- nostalgia simulation
- aesthetic shortcuts

Prioritize:

- believable image behavior
- lighting behavior
- atmosphere preservation
- physical texture
- emotional realism
- circulation realism

---

## Experiment philosophy

Experiments should:

1. isolate a variable
2. generate comparison outputs
3. create contact sheets
4. produce written observations
5. recommend the next experiment

Small experiments > giant systems.

---

## TRACE direction

TRACE is not a retro camera app.

TRACE is an attempt to preserve what phone photography removes.

Focus areas:

- shadow preservation
- mixed light behavior
- realistic falloff
- imperfect clarity
- subtle motion softness
- believable low light
- roll coherence
- memory realism

---

## Repository rules

- Do not modify Hermes core behavior unless necessary.
- All lab work should live under labs/.
- Experimental outputs should be timestamped.
- Preserve reproducibility.
- Log experiments clearly.
- Keep tooling modular and inspectable.
