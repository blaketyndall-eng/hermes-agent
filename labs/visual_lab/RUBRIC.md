# Visual Lab Scoring Rubric

All scoring uses a 1–5 scale.

The goal is not technical perfection.

The goal is believable image behavior.

---

# 1. Atmosphere

Measures whether the image preserves emotional mood, tension, darkness, distance, or environmental feeling.

## 1
Flat, sterile, emotionally empty.

## 2
Some mood present but weak or inconsistent.

## 3
Moderate atmosphere with believable tonal behavior.

## 4
Strong atmosphere with convincing environmental presence.

## 5
Deep emotional atmosphere. The image feels lived-in, remembered, or physically present.

---

# 2. Believability

Measures whether the image feels naturally captured rather than heavily processed.

## 1
Obviously filtered or artificial.

## 2
Processing is noticeable and distracting.

## 3
Mostly believable with occasional artificial behavior.

## 4
Natural-looking processing with subtle intervention.

## 5
Feels genuinely captured rather than edited.

---

# 3. Shadow Texture

Measures whether dark regions preserve physical texture and dimensionality.

## 1
Shadows are destroyed, muddy, or clipped.

## 2
Some texture remains but feels crushed or noisy.

## 3
Acceptable texture preservation.

## 4
Strong physical shadow behavior.

## 5
Dark regions feel dimensional, tactile, and realistic.

---

# 4. Phone Avoidance

Measures whether the image avoids modern smartphone over-processing behaviors.

## 1
Looks aggressively computational.

## 2
Heavy HDR or phone-like cleanup remains.

## 3
Partially avoids phone behavior.

## 4
Successfully avoids most smartphone artifacts.

## 5
Feels distinctly non-phone while remaining believable.

---

# 5. Memory Pressure

Measures whether the image feels emotionally remembered rather than clinically documented.

## 1
Purely informational image.

## 2
Minimal emotional weight.

## 3
Moderate sense of memory or emotional residue.

## 4
Strong remembered-image feeling.

## 5
Feels psychologically sticky or emotionally persistent.

---

# 6. Physical Light Logic

Measures whether light behaves in a believable physical way.

## 1
Light behavior feels fake or inconsistent.

## 2
Some believable lighting but obvious processing artifacts.

## 3
Generally realistic light behavior.

## 4
Strong physical lighting relationships.

## 5
Light feels naturally captured with convincing falloff and exposure behavior.

---

# 7. Subtlety

Measures whether processing remains restrained and difficult to notice directly.

## 1
Overprocessed and obvious.

## 2
Stylistic manipulation is easy to detect.

## 3
Moderately restrained.

## 4
Subtle processing with careful balance.

## 5
Nearly invisible intervention.

---

# 8. Roll Coherence

Measures whether a group of images feels like it belongs together.

## 1
No consistency between images.

## 2
Weak or accidental coherence.

## 3
Moderate shared visual behavior.

## 4
Strong roll-level continuity.

## 5
Images feel unified without appearing preset-driven.
