# Future TRACE Experiments

This document tracks possible future experiment directions.

The goal is not to simulate film.

The goal is to study believable image behavior.

---

# Experiment 002 — Falloff

Study:

- edge darkness
- center weighting
- uneven exposure
- environmental depth

Research question:

How much falloff increases atmosphere before becoming stylistically obvious?

---

# Experiment 003 — Mixed Light

Study:

- sodium vapor
- fluorescent spill
- tungsten warmth
- low-light color ambiguity

Research question:

How can TRACE preserve conflicting light temperatures without aggressive white balancing?

---

# Experiment 004 — Motion Softness

Study:

- slight blur
- handheld instability
- low-light movement
- imperfect sharpness

Research question:

How much softness feels believable before becoming unusable?

---

# Experiment 005 — Roll Coherence

Study:

- consistency across multiple images
- stable behavior patterns
- scene adaptation
- emotional continuity

Research question:

What makes a sequence of images feel like one memory object?

---

# Experiment 006 — Compression and Circulation

Study:

- screenshots
- repost chains
- upload degradation
- resized images
- platform damage

Research question:

How does circulation affect emotional realism?

---

# Experiment 007 — Flash Collapse

Study:

- foreground dominance
- harsh distance falloff
- black background collapse
- proximity pressure

Research question:

Why do direct-flash images often feel emotionally immediate?

---

# Experiment 008 — Surface Rendering

Study:

- skin
- fabric
- walls
- reflective surfaces
- dark clothing

Research question:

What texture behaviors feel physically believable without becoming hyper-detailed?
