# TRACE Ordinary-Life Dataset Philosophy

TRACE should focus on ordinary emotional environments.

Not:

- highly curated photography
- cinematic compositions
- influencer aesthetics
- hyper-polished imagery

The strongest TRACE territory is likely:

ordinary life under imperfect visual conditions.

---

# Recommended Environment Categories

## Parking Lots

Important behaviors:

- sodium vapor spill
- black distance collapse
- reflective asphalt
- isolated highlights

---

## Apartment Kitchens

Important behaviors:

- warm low-output bulbs
- muddy corners
- mixed appliance light
- soft environmental falloff

---

## Dive Bars

Important behaviors:

- dark clothing merge
- mixed red/yellow light
- handheld softness
- dimensional shadow

---

## Convenience Stores

Important behaviors:

- fluorescent overheads
- reflective packaging
- over-bright foregrounds
- environmental emptiness

---

## Cars at Night

Important behaviors:

- dashboard glow
- windshield reflections
- directional darkness
- isolated faces and hands

---

## Sidewalks and Late Walks

Important behaviors:

- uneven street lighting
- movement softness
- distant highlights
- environmental ambiguity

---

# Important Principle

TRACE should study:

emotionally ordinary images.

The goal is not:

spectacle.

The goal is:

believable remembered life.
