# TRACE Experiment Rubric

## 1. Atmosphere

Does the image preserve emotional mood and environmental feeling?

## 2. Believability

Does the image feel naturally captured rather than obviously processed?

## 3. Shadow Texture

Do dark regions preserve dimensionality and physical texture?

## 4. Phone Avoidance

Does the image avoid aggressive smartphone HDR and cleanup behavior?

## 5. Memory Pressure

Does the image feel remembered rather than clinically documented?
