# TRACE Run Review Workflow

The primary TRACE review surface is the contact sheet.

The goal is not to identify the most dramatic image.
The goal is to identify the most believable emotional behavior.

---

# Review Process

## Step 1 — Open contact sheets

Review all versions side-by-side.

Avoid zooming immediately.

First impressions matter.

Questions:

- Which image feels emotionally strongest?
- Which image feels least phone-like?
- Which image feels physically believable?
- Which image feels over-processed?

---

## Step 2 — Score outputs

Use:

```text
Atmosphere
Believability
Shadow Texture
Phone Avoidance
Memory Pressure
```

Do not prioritize technical sharpness.

---

## Step 3 — Identify recurring behaviors

Look for patterns across multiple images.

Examples:

- dark clothing collapse
- believable dimensional shadows
- fake texture appearance
- over-clean lifted shadows
- strong environmental atmosphere

---

## Step 4 — Review failures

Important:

Failure analysis is as valuable as successful outputs.

Document:

- muddy blacks
- artificial texture
- fake-looking contrast
- loss of subject detail
- overprocessed behavior

---

## Step 5 — Define next experiment

Every experiment should produce:

- observations
- questions
- next directions

The lab evolves through accumulated iteration.

---

# Key Principle

TRACE should optimize for:

- remembered feeling
- emotional realism
- physical light behavior
- restrained instability

Not spectacle.
