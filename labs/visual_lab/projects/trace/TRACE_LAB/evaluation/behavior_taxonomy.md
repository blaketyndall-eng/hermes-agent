# TRACE Behavior Taxonomy

This taxonomy labels what an image is doing visually and emotionally.

Avoid aesthetic labels like:

- vintage
- retro
- cinematic
- moody
- filmic

Prefer behavioral labels that can be observed, compared, and studied.

---

# Shadow Behaviors

## deep_shadow

Dark regions remain meaningfully dark.

## dimensional_black

Blacks feel physical and layered rather than flat or clipped.

## shadow_texture

Dark areas preserve subtle surface behavior.

## muddy_black

Dark areas collapse into lifeless digital mud.

## over_lifted_shadow

Shadows look clean, flat, and computational.

---

# Light Behaviors

## physical_falloff

Brightness falls away in a believable spatial pattern.

## foreground_dominance

Foreground subject carries the visual weight of the image.

## background_collapse

Background detail disappears or compresses into darkness.

## mixed_light_ambiguity

Conflicting light temperatures remain present instead of being normalized.

## highlight_anchor

A highlight acts as a visual memory anchor.

---

# Texture Behaviors

## restrained_instability

Subtle texture or softness increases realism without becoming an effect.

## obvious_texture

Texture becomes visible as processing.

## surface_physicality

Skin, fabric, wall, glass, or object surfaces feel tactile.

## over_clean_surface

Surfaces feel smoothed, denoised, or phone-cleaned.

---

# Motion Behaviors

## slight_motion_softness

Small motion blur or softness feels natural.

## unusable_blur

Blur damages the image beyond useful memory realism.

## handheld_presence

The image feels held by a person rather than stabilized by a device.

---

# Memory Behaviors

## memory_pressure_high

The image lingers emotionally or psychologically.

## remembered_not_documented

The image feels like a retained moment rather than a neutral record.

## emotional_ambiguity

The image leaves room for uncertainty or interpretation.

## ordinary_life_anchor

A mundane object or setting carries emotional weight.

---

# Phone Behaviors

## phone_hdr_flatness

The image feels aggressively lifted or uniformly visible.

## computational_cleanliness

The image feels denoised, sharpened, or polished by a phone pipeline.

## anti_phone_success

The image avoids phone-like processing while remaining believable.

---

# Circulation Behaviors

## circulation_softness

Compression or resizing creates subtle memory residue.

## repost_damage

The image shows signs of repeated digital circulation.

## archive_feeling

The image feels preserved from a prior context rather than freshly generated.
